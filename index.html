Cube Rush

  • High Concept: Jump over obstacles to the beat.
  • Player Experience Goals: Rhythm game with nice music and simple play.
  • Genre: Rhythm action.
  • Game play: Use the up arrow key to jump and the down key to restart. Avoid the red.
  • Prototype goal: Make a rhythm based game using tilemaps.
  • Features: Rhythm game using jump physics and simple aesthetic.
  • Setting/Premise: None.
  • Story: None.
  • Target Audience: Everyone 10 and up who has interest in music and rhythm based games.
  • Play time: 3-5 mins.
  • Strategies: Focus on the platforms.
